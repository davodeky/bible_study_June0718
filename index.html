Intentional Growth- Comparison gap

This Week's Scriptures

  • 1 Corinthians 13:11

Quick Pointers (Adapted from deeptruths.com)

  • Negative comparisons lead to discontentment, discouragement and jealousy.
    • 1 Samuel 18:8-9 ‐ Saul was angry at and jealous of David.
    • Acts 7:9 ‐ The patriachs sold Joseph into Egypt.
    • 2 Corinthians 10:12 ‐ Comparing is not wise.
  • Comparing ourselves favorably with others can lead to pride and self-righteousnes.
    • Luke 18:9-14 ‐ The Parable of the Pharisee and the Tax Collector
    • Romans 12:3 ‐ We should not think of ourselves more highly.
    • 1 Corinthians 4:7 ‐ We should not boast of the things we have received.
  • Comparing results from carnal mindedness and spiritual immaturity.
    • Romans 8:5 ‐ We should live according to the spirit.
    • 1 Corinthians 3:1-8‐ Envy, strife and divisions are carnal.
    • 2 Corinthians 5:12 ‐ We should take pride of what is in the heart.

Discussion Questions

  • Why do we compare ourselves?
  • In what ways and when do we compare ourselves to others?
  • How do we overcome the urge to compare ourselves to others?

3 ways to Overcome comparsion- the bible way(from rockthis.org)

  • Galatians 1:10 ‐ Our goal should not be to please others, but Christ.
  • Galatians 6:4‐ Our standard should be our own selves.
  • Romans 12:6‐ Comparing ourselves to others is trying to be who we are not.
